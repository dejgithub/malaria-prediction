💡 Prevention Strategies & Recommendations

🚀 Pre-Emergency Preparedness

For the high-risk year 2027, allocate additional resources 3-6 months before peak season. Stockpile antimalarial drugs and diagnostic kits.

🛡️ Vector Control Measures

Implement intensive mosquito control during rainy seasons. Distribute long-lasting insecticidal nets (LLINs) in endemic areas, especially before May.

🏥 Healthcare System Readiness

Increase staffing and bed capacity in hospitals during peak months (June-September). Deploy rapid diagnostic tests to remote areas.

🌡️ Environmental Interventions

Coordinate with weather services for early warning. Implement drainage projects in mosquito breeding areas before rainy season.

📢 Public Awareness Campaigns

Launch community education programs before peak seasons. Promote indoor residual spraying and personal protection measures.

🔬 Research & Monitoring

Continuous surveillance of drug resistance patterns. Monitor climate data for predictive modeling improvements.
